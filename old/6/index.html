Quick info

My name is Fivos and I'm a young, self-taught designer from Athens, Greece.

After studying (BSc) Management and (MSc) Logistics in the UK, I decided to make a U-turn and do what I actually wanted; I decided to turn my passion for pixel-perfect design into a career. Now, I work full-time at Longaccess and Deepfreeze.io as a UI/UX Designer and it's been the year of my life! I also work on various side projects, so if you have an awesome idea, I'd love to hear it and possibly work on it with you.

You can see some of my work on Dribbble, my photos on Flickr and Instagram and some design-related things I post on Twitter.

Shoot me an e-mail at: me@afivos.com
